Masterpiece Accommodation

On the Masterpiece trip, you'll sleep soundly in comfortable accommodations that we have chosen specifically for their fantastic locations. You’ll meet friendly hosts and have a private ensuite room each night.

Aoraki Alpine Lodge

Set in the heart of Aoraki Mount Cook National Park, the lodge is ideally situated for exploring the National Park’s many walks. The Mount Cook village itself is a lovely spot to enjoy the views with a barista-made coffee, or visit the Edmund Hillary Alpine Centre museum and planetarium. An internationally recognised Dark Sky Reserve, the night sky here is truly outstanding with myriads of stars visible to the naked eye. You’ll sleep well tonight in a comfortable ensuite room.

The Palms Motel Nelson

Located within a short walk to the centre of town, this smart hotel in sunny nelson makes for a happy stay. Your unit will have a fully kitted out kitchenette if you fancy cooking something up on your day off and a swimming pool, perfect for cooling off. There’s even a shared BBQ and dining area by the pool.

Lochmara Lodge (Queen Charlotte Sound, Marlborough Sounds)

This unique place is a lodge, wildlife recovery and arts centre so there is plenty to explore. You will arrive under your own steam by kayaking across the sound to the lodge beach, where you’ll be welcomed by the hosts and shown to your homely room. Explore the sculpture trail, visit the rescued wildlife and discover more about their conservation programmes before enjoying an excellent meal with an amazing view from the restaurant overlooking the Sound.

Edgewater Resort Wanaka

 

A stunning lakeside retreat, with charming apartment style accommodation, offering you the opportunity to relax and unwind in luxury after a busy day in the outdoors. Take advantage of the spa and sauna facilities before or after dinner, or take a walk along the lakefront track for incredible panoramic views of Lake Wanaka and the Southern Alps.

Please note, on occasion for operational reasons, we may stay at an alternative but equally attractive and comfortable accommodations.
